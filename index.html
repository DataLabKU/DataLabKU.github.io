Kuwait University · Computer Science

DATA Lab

Data Analytics, Trends & Applications Research Laboratory

We develop data-driven methods that bridge artificial intelligence, machine learning, network science, and natural language processing. Our work addresses problems across social, health, and linguistic domains, with a focus on building systems that are scalable, interpretable, and applicable to real-world challenges.

What we do

Research Areas

Arabic NLP

Computational methods for understanding and generating Arabic text, spanning a range of language understanding and generation tasks across diverse Arabic varieties.

Hypergraph Learning

Extending graph neural networks to higher-order relationships using hypergraph structures, with applications in social networks, behavioral modeling, and transaction graphs.

Social Media Analytics

Computational analysis of online behavior: bot identification, trend modeling, health misinformation, and community detection.

Streaming Graph Systems

Dynamic and continuous learning on evolving graphs, including temporal pattern detection and memory-efficient neural architectures.

Assistive Technology

Design and evaluation of technology-driven tools that support communication and accessibility, with emphasis on Arabic language interfaces for users with special needs.

Health-Related Analysis

Application of machine learning to public health and behavioral data, including mental health signal detection, misinformation spread, and population-level pattern analysis.
